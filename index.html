Learn to see the familiar world with fresh eyes. Discover how expertise can sometimes blind us, and how returning to a state of wonder unlocks hidden beauty in the ordinary.

About the Author

"I remember the morning I first understood the difference between looking and seeing. I was walking to work... then I noticed the way shadows fell between two buildings."

This moment of recognition sparked a journey that would eventually become How to See Differently. The author is not a guru on a mountaintop, but a companion on the path—someone who has discovered that the ordinary world is waiting to be seen as extraordinary.

Through these pages, they offer not a manual of rules, but a series of gentle invitations to wake up to the life you are already living.
