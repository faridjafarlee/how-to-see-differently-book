Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be an artist to read this?

Absolutely not. This book redefines creativity as a way of life, not just a skill for painters or musicians. It is for anyone who wants to live more consciously.

Is this a religious book?

No. While it is spiritual in the sense that it deals with meaning and connection, it is not tied to any specific religion or dogma.

What if I don't have time to practice?

The practices in this book are designed to be integrated into your existing day—while you walk, wash dishes, or drink coffee. They don't require extra time, just extra attention.

About Farid Jafarli

"I remember the morning I first understood the difference between looking and seeing. I was walking to work... then I noticed the way shadows fell between two buildings."

This moment of recognition sparked a journey that would eventually become How to See Differently. Farid is not a guru on a mountaintop, but a companion on the path—someone who has discovered that the ordinary world is waiting to be seen as extraordinary.

Through these pages, Farid offers not a manual of rules, but a series of gentle invitations to wake up to the life you are already living.
